About Our Project

Our mission is to reduce accidents caused by drowsy driving by utilizing cutting-edge machine learning technologies to detect drowsiness in real-time.

Why Drowsiness Detection?

Drowsy driving is a leading cause of accidents, with thousands of lives lost each year. Our system helps detect early signs of drowsiness and alerts the driver before it's too late.
